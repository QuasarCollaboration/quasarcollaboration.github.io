Current Projects

Explore the cutting-edge research and collaborative initiatives led by our members.

Exoplanet Atmosphere Survey

Using the Mt Kent Observatory to characterize the atmospheres of Hot Jupiters, searching for water vapor and other biomarkers.

Lead: USQ Active

Gravitational Wave Alert Pipeline

Developing real-time data processing pipelines for the LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA collaboration to identify optical counterparts.

Lead: UQ & QUT Development

Cosmological Simulations

Large-scale N-body simulations to model galaxy formation and dark matter distribution in the early universe.

Lead: UQ Active
