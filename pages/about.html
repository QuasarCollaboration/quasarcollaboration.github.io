About QUASAR

The QUASAR Collaboration represents a groundbreaking partnership between Queensland's leading universitiesâ€”bringing together expertise in astronomy, astrophysics, and space science to advance research, education, and outreach across the region.

Our Mission

To foster collaborative excellence in astronomical research and education by leveraging the combined strengths of University of Queensland, Queensland University of Technology, and University of Southern Queensland.

Our Vision

Positioning Queensland as a premier destination for astronomical research and public engagement with the cosmos, while nurturing the next generation of astronomers and space scientists.

Key Objectives

  • Facilitate joint research programs and grant applications
  • Share facilities and computational resources
  • Coordinate graduate student training and exchanges
  • Organize collaborative workshops and seminars
  • Engage the public through outreach activities
  • Host major astronomical conferences and meetings
